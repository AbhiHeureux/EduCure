Features

Everything Your School Needs

Purpose-built tools for every department — no juggling multiple apps.

Fee Management

Define multiple fee heads (tuition, transport, hostel, lab), set class-wise installment schedules, collect via UPI or cash, auto-generate numbered receipts, apply sibling/merit concessions, and send WhatsApp reminders to defaulters — fully reconciled in real time.

Attendance Tracking

Mark class-wise attendance in under 60 seconds, integrate biometric devices, auto-notify parents on absence via SMS or WhatsApp, flag students below 75% for detainment review, and generate consolidated monthly reports per class or subject.

Exam & Report Cards

Configure Unit Tests, Half-Yearly, and Finals. Supports CBSE CCE grading (A1–E), ICSE marks, and State Board formats. Subject teachers enter marks directly; EduCare auto-calculates total, grade, rank, and co-scholastic areas — bulk PDF report cards ready to print in one click.

Admissions

Capture online enquiries with source tracking (walk-in, referral, social media), collect registration fees, maintain document checklists (Birth Certificate, TC, Aadhaar, caste certificate), link siblings automatically, and bulk-promote all students to the next class at year-end in seconds.

Staff Management

Maintain complete staff profiles with qualification and joining details, process monthly payroll with PF, ESI, and TDS deductions, generate payslips and bank advice reports, manage leave applications with an approval workflow, and track daily staff attendance — biometric-ready.

Library Management

Catalog books by title, author, subject, and accession number. Issue and return using barcode scanning, auto-calculate fines on overdue books, send alerts before due date, restrict borrowing for fee defaulters, and generate annual stock registers for audits.

How It Works

Up and Running in Minutes

No IT team needed. EduCare is simple enough for any school staff to use from day one.

1

Configure Your School

Enter school details, set up the academic year, create classes (I–XII), sections (A, B, C), and define fee structures — class-wise, installment-wise, head-wise. Takes under 30 minutes.

2

Import Students & Staff

Bulk-import students via Excel or add individually. Assign subjects to teachers, configure timetables, and set role-based permissions — Admin, Accountant, Class Teacher, or custom roles.

3

Run Your School Daily

Collect fees, mark attendance, enter marks after exams — EduCare auto-generates reports, sends parent WhatsApp alerts on absence or dues, and keeps all your data backed up securely in the cloud.

Why EduCare

Built for Schools, Not Just Businesses

Most ERPs are generic. EduCare is designed from the ground up for how Indian schools actually work.

Cloud-Based — No Installation Needed

Access EduCare from any browser on any device — laptop, tablet, or phone. No server to buy, no software to install. Schools are live within a day, not weeks.

WhatsApp & SMS Built-In

Send fee reminders, absence alerts, exam results, and event notices directly to parents via WhatsApp or SMS — no third-party integration, no per-message setup. Parents respond in real time.

CBSE, ICSE & State Board Ready

Pre-configured grading for CBSE CCE (A1–E), ICSE subject marking patterns, and State Board formats. Switch grading rules per class — no workarounds, no spreadsheets.

Your Records Are Always Safe

Automated daily backups, AES-256 encrypted storage, and granular role-based access control — so the right staff see only what they need, and your decade of school data is never at risk.

Modules

One Platform, Every Department

8 integrated modules — data entered once flows everywhere. Fees auto-link to admissions. Attendance auto-alerts parents. Marks auto-populate report cards.

Dashboard & Analytics

Real-time KPIs — today's attendance %, pending fee amounts, upcoming exams, and recent admissions. Drill-down charts for class-wise attendance trends, month-wise fee collection, and subject-wise exam performance.

Expense Tracking

Record school expenses by category (maintenance, stationery, salary, utilities), attach vouchers, compare budget vs. actuals month-over-month, and export reports for audits.

Certificate Generation

Generate Bonafide, Transfer Certificates, Character Certificates, and student/staff ID cards in one click. School letterhead, principal signature, and seal auto-populated — print-ready A4/ID-card formats.

Payroll Management

Calculate gross and net salaries with PF, ESI, TDS, and advance deductions. Generate individual payslips, bank NEFT advice files, and Form 16 summaries — all mapped to your staff attendance records.

Fee Reports

Daily collection register, class-wise fee ledger, outstanding defaulters list, concession and discount summaries, and head-wise breakdowns — all exportable to Excel or PDF for management or trust review.

Role-based Access Control

Control exactly what each staff member can see and do — 50+ granular permissions. Accountant sees fees only; Class Teacher sees their section only; Principal sees everything.

Timetable Management

Create class-wise and teacher-wise timetables, assign subject teachers to each period, auto-detect scheduling clashes, and share digital timetables with staff instantly.

Transport Management

Define bus routes and stops, assign students to vehicles, collect route-wise transport fees as a separate fee head, track daily vehicle attendance, and alert parents on bus delays.
