Features

Everything Your School Needs

Purpose-built tools for every department — no juggling multiple apps.

Fee Management

Collect fees, generate receipts, track defaulters, apply concessions, and view daily/monthly collection reports — all in real time.

Attendance Tracking

Mark student & staff attendance daily, send parent SMS alerts, generate reports, and monitor trends with visual analytics.

Exam & Report Cards

Configure exams, enter marks, auto-generate report cards with grades, ranks, and remarks. Print-ready in one click.

Admissions
